L'ANFR recommande aux utilisateurs d'installer cette mise à jour pour éviter notamment des brouillages de radars météorologiques.
EN Summary: The French National Frequency Agency (ANFR) announces that Ubiquiti Networks Inc. has updated its LiteAP AC GPS wifi router to correct non-compliance with radio frequency usage conditions. Inspections found the router to be non-compliant, but a software update has resolved the issue, ensuring compliant use of the radio spectrum. The ANFR advises users to install this update to prevent interference with weather radars, among other risks.

EN Summary: In 2025, the French National Frequencies Agency (ANFR) tested 80 mobile devices for compliance with electromagnetic wave exposure limits (SAR). Nine devices exceeded regulatory limits, specifically for limb and head SAR, while eight had administrative non-conformities. Seventeen models required corrective actions, ranging from software updates to market withdrawal.
KO Summary: 2025년 프랑스 국립주파수기관(ANFR)은 전자파 노출 한도(SAR) 준수 여부를 확인하기 위해 80개의 모바일 기기를 검사했습니다. 9개 기기는 사지 및 머리 SAR에서 규제 한도를 초과했으며, 8개는 행정적 부적합 사항을 보였습니다. 17개 모델은 소프트웨어 업데이트부터 시장 철수까지 시정 조치가 필요했습니다.

FR Summary: L'ANFR a ordonné le retrait et le rappel du téléphone NUBIA Z70 ULTRA – NX733J du marché français en raison de dépassements de la limite de débit d'absorption spécifique (DAS) "membre". Ce contrôle vise à garantir la protection du public contre l'exposition aux ondes électromagnétiques.

GOLDEN STREAM is required to take corrective actions, and if they fail to do so, distributors must initiate the withdrawal and recall themselves. This measure ensures public protection against electromagnetic wave exposure.

This update from the ANFR observatory confirms the gradual phasing out of 2G and 3G networks.
